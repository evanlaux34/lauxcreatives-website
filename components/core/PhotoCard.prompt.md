The signature Laux "taped print" — a photo resting on an ivory mat with a soft warm drop-shadow and a contact-sheet caption. Used for nearly all imagery.

```jsx
<PhotoCard src="assets/photos/IMG_9828.jpg" caption="the whole party in motion" fig="Fig. 01" height={420} />
<PhotoCard src="..." tone="bw" caption="Portraits" onDark />
```

Tones `warm | sepia | bw`. Set `onDark` on dark sections for the deeper shadow. Lifts 2px on hover.
